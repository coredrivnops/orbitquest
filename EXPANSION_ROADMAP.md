## Phase 2: "THE HEART OF THE SYSTEM" - The Sun

### Unlock Condition
> After reaching Earth AND earning **1,000+ total Stardust**, a new mission appears:
> 
> *"Warning: Solar activity detected. Your probe's sensors have picked up an anomaly at the heart of the solar system. Investigate the Sun to unlock its secrets..."*

### üåü THE SUN: "Solar Storm Survivor"

#### Educational Focus
- The Sun's corona (2 million¬∞K - hotter than its surface!)
- Solar flares and their X-ray classifications
- Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs) - billions of tons of plasma
- Why the corona is hotter than the surface (unsolved mystery!)
- The 11-year solar cycle

#### Game Concept: **Corona Run** 
**Mechanic:** Survival bullet-hell with shield management

**Gameplay:**
- Player controls the Parker Solar Probe approaching the Sun
- Navigate through the corona, dodging solar flares shooting outward
- Collect "solar particles" for points while avoiding CME waves
- **Heat Shield Mechanic:** Shield degrades over time, must find "shadow zones" behind sunspots to cool down
- **Phase transitions:** As you get closer, the game speeds up and hazards intensify
- **Magnetic field lines:** Visual guides that help or hinder movement
- **X-class flare events:** Screen-wide warnings before massive attacks

**Unique Animations:**
- Dynamic plasma effects with particle systems
- Heat distortion shader on screen edges as temperature rises
- Corona streaming effects using animated gradients
- Solar prominence eruptions as animated obstacles
- Sunspot rotation (Sun rotates differently at equator vs poles!)

**Controls:** Mouse/touch to move, shield recharges in shadow zones

## Phase 3: "THE VOID BETWEEN" - Black Hole Gateway

### Unlock Condition
> After completing the Sun AND earning **2,500+ total Stardust**:
> 
> *"Probe Alert: A gravitational anomaly has been detected. Analysis suggests a stellar-mass black hole at the edge of our system. This could be your gateway to the stars beyond..."*

### üï≥Ô∏è BLACK HOLE: "Event Horizon"

#### Educational Focus
- What is a black hole? (Collapsed massive star)
- The Event Horizon - point of no return
- Spaghettification - tidal forces stretching objects
- Time dilation near black holes (time slows down!)
- Hawking radiation and black hole evaporation
- Gravitational lensing - light bending around black holes

#### Game Concept: **Spaghetti Run**
**Mechanic:** Perspective-warping endless runner with time dilation

**Gameplay:**
- Player probe approaches the black hole
- Must navigate the "ergosphere" - the region where spacetime itself is dragged
- **Time dilation zones:** Purple areas where time slows, giving you more reaction time but also slowing score accumulation
- **Stretched perspective:** As you get closer, the screen warps (gravitational lensing effect)
- **Accretion disk surfing:** Ride the swirling disk of matter, dodge debris
- **Spaghettification warning:** Get too close and your probe stretches visually before game over
- **Hawking particles:** Rare collectibles at the edge worth bonus points
- Goal: Reach "warp velocity" to slingshot through the black hole

**Unique Animations:**
- Real-time gravitational lensing shader (stars behind distort)
- Accretion disk with orange-red hot matter spiraling
- Probe stretching animation near event horizon
- Time dilation visual effect (world goes purple/slow-mo)
- Particle fountain of Hawking radiation at edges

**Upon Completion:** Portal opens to the **Galactic Map** - new worlds beyond our solar system!

## Phase 4: "DISTANT SHORES" - Exoplanet Exploration

After escaping through the black hole, players unlock access to **THREE STAR SYSTEMS**, each requiring increasing Stardust to unlock.

### üåç System 1: PROXIMA CENTAURI (Nearest Star: 4.2 light-years)
*Unlock Cost: 500‚≠ê*

#### Planet A: **Proxima Centauri b** - "Flare Runner"

**Educational Focus:**
- Closest exoplanet to Earth
- Tidally locked (one side always faces its star)
- Orbits a red dwarf that has violent flare events
- Possible water on the terminator line (day/night boundary)

**Game Concept: Terminator Zone**
**Mechanic:** Side-scrolling auto-runner on a rotating planet

**Gameplay:**
- Play on the terminator line (edge between light and dark)
- Planet slowly rotates - you must stay in the "sweet spot"
- Too close to day side = overheating from flares
- Too close to night side = freezing cold
- Dodge solar flare events that randomly strike the day side
- Collect ice crystals from night side, fire gems from day side
- Balance temperature meter in the middle

**Unique Animations:**
- Half-screen warm (orange/red), half-screen cold (blue/purple)
- Dynamic flare bursts from red dwarf
- Terminator line shimmer effect
- Temperature wave distortions

### ‚≠ê System 2: TRAPPIST-1 (40 light-years away)
*Unlock Cost: 1,000‚≠ê*

#### Overview
- 7 Earth-sized planets orbiting an ultra-cool red dwarf
- Planets appear as large as the Moon from each other's surface
- 3 planets in habitable zone
- All likely tidally locked

#### Planet A: **TRAPPIST-1e** - "Orbital Dance"

**Educational Focus:**
- In the habitable zone
- Possible liquid water
- Close orbital resonance with siblings (gravitational harmony)
- Neighboring planets visible as large disks in sky

**Game Concept: Gravity Orchestra**
**Mechanic:** Rhythm-based orbital navigation

**Gameplay:**
- Control a probe hopping between TRAPPIST-1 planets
- Each planet has a "beat" based on its orbital period
- Jump in rhythm to slingshot between planets
- Miss the beat = crash or miss the orbital window
- Collect samples from each planet as you hop
- Boss phase: All 7 planets align, creating a gravitational surge

**Unique Animations:**
- Seven planets in view, each with distinct appearance
- Orbital trail lines showing resonance patterns
- Musical notes emanating when you sync correctly
- Planetary transits (planets crossing each other)

#### Planet B: **GJ 9827 d** - "Steam World"

**Educational Focus:**
- First confirmed "steam world"
- Atmosphere almost entirely water vapor
- Twice the size of Earth

**Game Concept: Vapor Ascent**
**Mechanic:** Vertical climber in steam atmosphere

**Gameplay:**
- Navigate upward through turbulent water vapor clouds
- Steam vents boost you higher
- Condensation zones (fog) reduce visibility
- Collect hydrogen and oxygen molecules
- Avoid lightning from charged clouds
- Goal: Reach the upper atmosphere before pressure crushes you

**Unique Animations:**
- Dense fog with dynamic visibility
- Steam particle effects
- Water droplet condensation on screen
- Pressure gauge increasing

#### Planet B: **HD 63433 d** (Half-Lava World) - "Day/Night Dash"

**Educational Focus:**
- Half the planet covered in bubbling lava
- Only 400 million years old (young planet!)
- Year lasts only 4 Earth days
- Likely no atmosphere

**Game Concept: Binary Sprint**
**Mechanic:** Two-phase runner that switches between environments

**Gameplay:**
- Run across the planet's surface as it rotates
- Day side: Molten lava, jump over cracks, avoid geysers
- Night side: Solidified rock, dodge meteor impacts (no atmosphere = direct hits)
- Transition zones: Screen shifts from orange to dark
- Collect stardust before it melts or freezes
- Speed run to complete an orbit before your probe overheats

**Unique Animations:**
- Dramatic day/night color shift
- Molten lava bubbling on day side
- Meteor shower on night side
- Probe heat gauge changing based on side

## Phase 5: "THE COSMIC LIGHTHOUSES" - Neutron Stars & Pulsars
*Unlock Cost: 5,000‚≠ê*

### ‚ö° PULSAR: "Lighthouse Labyrinth"

**Educational Focus:**
- Rapidly rotating neutron stars
- Emit beams of radiation from magnetic poles
- Spin up to 716 times per SECOND
- More accurate than atomic clocks
- A teaspoon weighs 6 billion tons!

**Game Concept: Beam Dodger**
**Mechanic:** Circular arena survival with rotating beam hazards

**Gameplay:**
- Arena rotates around a central pulsar
- Twin beams sweep across the arena at regular intervals
- Time your movements between beam sweeps
- Beams speed up as pulsar "spins faster" (difficulty increases)
- Collect neutron particles near the pulsar core
- Use gravitational assist zones to slingshot around

**Unique Animations:**
- Twin beam lighthouse effect
- Screen shake from gravitational waves
- Extreme density visual compression near core
- Radio wave pulse indicators

---

### üß≤ MAGNETAR: "Magnetic Mayhem"

**Educational Focus:**
- Strongest magnets in the universe
- Magnetic field can erase credit cards from halfway to the Moon
- Cause "starquakes" - crust shifts that release gamma-ray bursts
- Can briefly outshine entire galaxies

**Game Concept: Pole Flip**
**Mechanic:** Polarity-switching platformer

**Gameplay:**
- Control a magnetized probe near a magnetar
- Switch between North and South polarity
- Matching polarity = repelled (pushed away)
- Opposite polarity = attracted (pulled toward)
- Navigate maze of magnetic field lines
- Dodge "starquake" shockwaves
- Collect exotic particles while managing magnetic flux

**Unique Animations:**
- Visible magnetic field lines pulsing
- Polarity indicator on probe (red/blue glow)
- Gamma-ray burst flashes
- Matter streaming along field lines
